📝 三大题型模板

议论文 观点论证型 · 三段式 ▼
第一段 · 引出话题 + 表明立场

Nowadays, there is a growing concern over [话题]. Some people argue that [观点A], while others maintain that [观点B]. As far as I am concerned, I firmly believe that [你的立场].

第二段 · 论证理由（2-3点）

To begin with, [理由1]. A compelling example is that [举例]. Furthermore, [理由2]. According to a recent survey, [数据/调查]. Last but not least, [理由3].

第三段 · 总结 + 建议

Taking all these factors into consideration, we may safely draw the conclusion that [重申立场]. Therefore, it is imperative that [建议/措施]. Only in this way can we [美好愿景].

议论文 利弊分析型 · 平衡式 ▼
第一段 · 引出话题 + 承认两面性

With the rapid development of modern society, [话题] has aroused widespread public attention. Like a double-edged sword, it brings both benefits and potential drawbacks.

第二段 · 分析利与弊

On the one hand, [好处1]. For instance, [例子]. On the other hand, [坏处1]. A typical example is that [例子]. Therefore, we should adopt a balanced perspective.

第三段 · 表明立场 + 总结

From my perspective, the advantages of [话题] outweigh its disadvantages, provided that [前提条件]. All in all, only by [建议] can we maximize its benefits while minimizing its negative impact.

议论文 问题解决型 · 现象-原因-对策 ▼
第一段 · 描述现象

In recent years, the issue of [社会现象] has become increasingly severe. It is not uncommon to see that [具体表现], which has raised great concern among the public.

第二段 · 分析原因

A number of factors can account for this phenomenon. To start with, [原因1]. In addition, [原因2]. Most importantly, [原因3] has made the situation even worse.

第三段 · 提出建议

To tackle this problem, effective measures should be taken from multiple perspectives. For individuals, [个人措施]. For the government and relevant authorities, [政府措施]. Only through joint efforts can we expect a brighter future.

议论文 对比选择型 · A or B ▼
第一段 · 引出争议

When it comes to [话题], opinions vary considerably. Some individuals prefer [选项A], whereas others are in favor of [选项B]. Personally, I would choose [你的选择] for the following reasons.

第二段 · 对比论证

The primary reason is that [理由1]. Compared with [另一选项], [你的选择] can [具体优势]. Moreover, [理由2]. It is evident that [总结优势].

第三段 · 总结重申

In conclusion, despite the merits of [另一选项], I still firmly support [你的选择] because [一句话总结]. Therefore, [建议/号召].

议论文 名言哲理型 · 引语阐释 ▼
第一段 · 引用名言 + 解释含义

The well-known saying "[名言]" has been widely accepted for generations. In my understanding, it conveys the idea that [阐释含义]. This proverb is particularly relevant in today's rapidly changing world.

第二段 · 举例论证

Numerous examples can be found to illustrate this point. [例子1]. Another convincing example is that [例子2]. These cases clearly demonstrate that [回扣名言].

第三段 · 联系自身 + 总结

As college students, we should bear this wisdom in mind and apply it to our daily life. [具体做法]. In a nutshell, [重申名言意义] is the key to [美好结果].

图表作文 数据描述 + 原因分析 + 趋势预测 ▼
第一段 · 描述图表

As is clearly illustrated in the chart/graph, [主要趋势]. The data shows that [具体数字] increased/decreased from [X] to [Y] during the period from [起始年] to [结束年]. Particularly noteworthy is the fact that [突出数据].

第二段 · 分析原因

Several factors contribute to this phenomenon. First and foremost, [原因1]. In addition, [原因2]. Moreover, [原因3] also plays a significant role in shaping this trend.

第三段 · 预测 + 建议

Based on the analysis above, it is reasonable to predict that [趋势预测]. To address this issue, [建议] should be taken. With joint efforts, [积极展望].

书信 建议信/投诉信/求职信 通用框架 ▼
开头 · 表明身份 + 写信目的

Dear [称谓],

I am writing this letter to [表达目的：express my concern / offer some suggestions / apply for]. I believe that [核心观点].

正文 · 具体内容（分点）

For one thing, [要点1]. For another, [要点2]. Meanwhile, I would like to suggest that [建议].

结尾 · 期待回复 + 礼貌收束

I sincerely hope that you will take my suggestions into consideration. I am looking forward to your favorable reply at your earliest convenience.

Yours sincerely,
[你的名字]

⚠️ 写作常见误区

1

中式英语直译

❌ "I very like English" → ✅ "I am very fond of English"

❌ "open the light" → ✅ "turn on the light"

2

主谓不一致

❌ "The number of students are increasing" → ✅ "The number of students is increasing"

注意：the number of 做主语谓语用单数，a number of 用复数

3

时态混乱

议论文描述普遍现象用一般现在时，举例过去的事用一般过去时，不要混用。

4

流水句（Run-on sentence）

❌ "I like reading it is interesting"

✅ "I like reading, which is interesting." 或用分号、连词连接

5

词汇重复

一篇作文出现 5 次 "important"，考官会觉得词汇量不足。用同义替换解决。

6

模板痕迹过重

背模板不等于照搬，要根据题目灵活调整。阅卷老师一眼能看出"生搬硬套"的模板。

🎯 30分钟写作策略

0-3 min

审题 + 列提纲

确定题型（议论文/图表/书信）→ 圈关键词 → 列出 2-3 个论点 → 想好开头结尾

3-10 min

写第一段

引出话题（1-2句）→ 表明立场（1句）→ 约 40-50 词。开门见山，别绕弯。

10-22 min

写主体段

每个论点用 Topic sentence + Explanation + Example 结构。2-3 个论点，每点 3-4 句。注意衔接词！

22-27 min

写结尾段

总结观点（换种说法）+ 建议/展望。结尾用倒装句或强调句型加分。约 30-40 词。

27-30 min

检查修改

主谓一致？时态正确？拼写错误？字数够了吗（150-200词）？字迹工整！

💡 加分小技巧

  • 用倒装句：Only by doing so can we... / Not only... but also...
  • 用强调句：It is... that... / It was not until... that...
  • 用虚拟语气：If it were not for..., ...would... / It is high time that...
  • 用非谓语动词：Determined to..., he... / Compared with..., ...
  • 用定语从句：..., which is of great significance
  • 字数控制在 180-200 最安全，太少扣分，太多浪费时间

📏 写作评分标准（满分15分）

档次分数标准
13-15优秀切题，表达思想清楚，文字通顺，连贯性好，基本无语言错误，仅有个别小错
10-12良好切题，表达思想清楚，文字连贯，有少量语言错误
7-9中等基本切题，有些地方思想表达不够清楚，文字勉强连贯，语言错误相当多
4-6及格基本切题，表达思想不清楚，连贯性差，有较多严重语言错误
1-3不及格条理不清，思想紊乱，语言支离破碎，大部分句子有错误

🎯 阅卷老师最看重的3件事

  • 结构清晰：三段式，每段主题明确，段首句即观点。阅卷老师 30 秒定档，结构第一印象最重要。
  • 语言准确：主谓一致、时态正确、拼写无误。3个以上严重语法错误直接降档。
  • 内容充实：有具体例证，不做空泛论述。"For example" 后面跟一个具体场景，比说一堆大道理有效。
